Staff Information

Quotas and Activity Requirements

Basic Requirements

• Each team has their own specific quota requirements
• Quota completion is mandatory to avoid inactivity termination
• Discord activity is required for promotion eligibility
• LOA requests available (max 2 weeks, extendable for severe cases)

Staff Conduct Guidelines

Proper Call Response

Example of correct greeting:

"Hello! My name Is [Name] and I'm a (rank) here at ATV. How can I help you today?"

Avatar Regulations

• Must maintain realistic and appropriate ROBLOX avatar
• No over-the-top accessories or features
• Must wear provided staff uniform while on duty
• Violations may result in punishment

Off-Duty Rules

• Maintain professional behavior even when off-duty
• No command usage while off-duty
• Must follow server rules at all times
• Must follow Roblox and Discord TOS

Moderation Guidelines

Zero Tolerance Offenses (Permanent Ban)

• Racism/Discrimination
• Terrorism/Violent Extremism
• Bypassing Chat Filters
• Exploiting
• NSFW Actions
• Mass Random Death Match

Three-Strike Offenses (Warning → Warning → Kick)

• Fear Roleplay
• Random Death Match
• Spawn Killing
• Vehicle Deathmatch
• Fail Roleplay
• Cuff Rushing/Auto Arresting

Escalating Offenses

• Harassment: Kick → Ban → Permanent
• Disrespect to Staff: Warning → Kick → Ban
• Harmful Glitching: Warning → Kick → Ban
• Major RDM/VDM (Staff): Kick → Permanent Ban

Operational Guidelines

Moderator Caps

10-15 Players: 3 Staff Maximum
15-30 Players: 4 Staff Maximum
30-40 Players: 5+ Staff Maximum

Peacetime Protocol

• Activate using :pt (seconds)
• Maximum recommended duration: 200 seconds
• Use when staff are overwhelmed with calls
• Keep duration reasonable to maintain player engagement

Retirement Process

• Open a ticket to request retirement
• All roles will be removed upon approval
• Cannot return to original rank after retirement
